# User Persona – MedBrief

### Basic Details

* Age: 38
* Profession: Cardiologist
* Location: Delhi, India
* Experience: 10+ years
* Work Environment: Private hospital + OPD

### Goals

* Stay updated with latest clinical research
* Quickly understand key findings without reading full papers
* Get alerts for important trials and guidelines
* Save relevant studies for later reference

* Lack of time to read full research papers
* Information overload from multiple sources

* Skims content instead of deep reading

### Needs from MedBrief

* Quick summaries (under 1–2 minutes)
* Personalized feed based on specialty
* Audio brief for multitasking
* Reliable and credible insights

### Key Features Used

* Morning Brief (Audio)
* Top Studies
* Bookmark
* Notifications (Trial + Safety Alerts)
